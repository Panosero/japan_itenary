Cultural Etiquette

• Bow when greeting - a slight nod is perfect

• Say "Itadakimasu" before eating

• Say "Gochisousama" after eating

• Remove shoes when you see others doing so

• Never tip - it's considered offensive!

• Keep voices low on trains

• Stand on the left on escalators (right in Osaka)
